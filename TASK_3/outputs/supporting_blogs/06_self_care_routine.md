## Daily Self-Care Habits (10 Minutes or Less)

### Morning Skincare
Keep it simple: cleanser, moisturiser with SPF. Hyderabad's sun is intense year-round — SPF is non-negotiable, not optional. This takes 3 minutes.

### Hair Care
Apply a leave-in conditioner or light hair serum on damp hair before stepping out. This one step significantly reduces frizz and breakage over time — especially important in Hyderabad's humidity.

### Hydration
Drink water before your coffee. Skin and hair health are deeply connected to internal hydration — no serum can fix chronic dehydration.

---

## Weekly Self-Care Habits (30–60 Minutes)

### Deep Conditioning Hair Mask
Once a week, apply a nourishing hair mask and leave it on for 20–30 minutes before washing. This rebuilds moisture and reduces the damage caused by heat styling and pollution.

### Face Mask or Exfoliation
A gentle exfoliation or sheet mask once a week keeps your skin clearer and more receptive to your daily products. Don't over-exfoliate — once a week is enough for most skin types.

### Digital Detox Evening
One evening per week — no screens for 60 minutes before bed. This improves sleep quality more than any eye cream will.

## Hyderabad-Specific Self-Care Tips

**Sun protection is essential.** Hyderabad's UV index is high for most of the year. Reapply SPF every 2–3 hours if you're outdoors.

**Humidity affects hair differently.** Hyderabad's monsoon season particularly wrecks hair that isn't properly maintained. A keratin or smoothening treatment once or twice a year can make your entire hair routine easier.

**Pollution is real.** Double cleansing your face (oil cleanser + water-based cleanser) at night removes pollutants that regular cleansing misses.
