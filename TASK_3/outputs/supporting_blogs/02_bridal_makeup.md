## What's Typically Included in a Bridal Makeup Package in Hyderabad

A complete bridal makeup package at a professional salon in Hyderabad should include more than just makeup on the wedding day. Here's what to look for:

### Wedding Day Makeup & Hair
Full bridal makeup including base, eyes, lips, and contouring — suited to your skin tone and ceremony type. Hair styling with or without extensions, depending on your look.

### Pre-Bridal Skin Prep Sessions
Most reputable salons recommend 2–4 pre-bridal facial sessions starting 4–6 weeks before the wedding. This preps your skin for a smoother, longer-lasting makeup base.

### Makeup Trial Session
A trial run of your complete bridal look — ideally done 2–3 weeks before the wedding. This is non-negotiable. Never skip the trial.

## How to Choose a Bridal Makeup Artist in Hyderabad

Not every salon that offers bridal services is the right fit for your wedding. Here's how to evaluate your options:

**Check their portfolio** — Look for before/after photos across different skin tones. A good artist can work on fair, wheatish, and deeper skin tones equally well.

**Ask about the products used** — HD makeup, airbrush, and long-wear foundations are standard for bridal work. Ask specifically which brands they use.

**Confirm who will do your makeup** — At some salons, the senior artist you consult with is not the one who shows up on your wedding day. Confirm this in writing.

**Read real reviews** — Focus on reviews that mention how the makeup held up through the ceremony, not just how it looked in the parlour.
