## Understanding Indian Skin Types

Before choosing a facial, it helps to know your skin category:

### Oily & Acne-Prone Skin
Very common in Hyderabad's humid climate. Skin produces excess sebum, leading to clogged pores and breakouts. Best treated with deep cleansing facials, salicylic acid-based treatments, and oil-control masks.

### Dry & Dull Skin
Often caused by dehydration, sun exposure, or harsh products. Skin feels tight, looks lackluster, and may flake. Best treated with hydrating facials, vitamin C, and hyaluronic acid-based treatments.

### Combination Skin
Oily in the T-zone (forehead, nose, chin), dry elsewhere. Needs a balanced approach — typically a customised facial that addresses both concerns.

### Hyperpigmented / Uneven Tone
Extremely common in Indian skin due to sun exposure and post-acne marks. Best addressed with brightening facials, chemical exfoliation, and ingredients like Niacinamide and Kojic Acid.

## Best Facial Treatments for Indian Skin

### Vitamin C Brightening Facial
Targets: Dullness, dark spots, uneven tone
How it works: High-concentration Vitamin C serum is applied after deep cleansing to brighten skin and fade pigmentation. Results are visible after 2–3 sessions.
Best for: Wheatish to deeper skin tones with pigmentation concerns.

### Deep Cleansing Facial
Targets: Clogged pores, blackheads, oily skin
How it works: Steam + extractions + purifying mask to clear congestion and reduce breakout frequency.
Best for: Oily and acne-prone skin types, especially in humid cities like Hyderabad.

### Hydrafacial
Targets: Dehydration, fine lines, overall dullness
How it works: Multi-step treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, and infuses skin with hydrating serums simultaneously.
Best for: All skin types — especially effective before a big occasion.

### Anti-Ageing Facial
Targets: Fine lines, loss of firmness, dull mature skin
How it works: Collagen-boosting ingredients, peptides, and massage techniques to firm and plump skin.
Best for: Women 30+ noticing early signs of ageing.

**Q: How often should I get a facial in Hyderabad?**

**Q: Are chemical facials safe for Indian skin?**

## 7 Things to Check Before Booking a Salon in Hyderabad

### 1. Hygiene Standards
The most important factor — and the easiest to spot. Look for clean tools, fresh towels, covered product containers, and disposable items for treatments like threading and waxing. A good salon won't cut corners here.

### 2. Stylist Qualifications
Ask whether stylists are certified or trained at a reputed institution. In Hyderabad's better salons, stylists often have certifications from brands like Wella, L'Oréal, or VLCC. Don't be afraid to ask.

### 3. Products Used
The products a salon uses directly affect your results and hair/skin health. Professional-grade brands cost more — but you shouldn't be paying premium prices for diluted or generic products.

### 4. Online Reviews — The Right Way to Read Them
Look for recent reviews (last 6 months). Focus on reviews that mention specific services, specific stylists, and results over time — not just "great place!" compliments.

### 5. Transparent Pricing
A trustworthy salon will tell you the price before starting — not after. Ask for a price list or quote upfront. Be cautious of salons that give vague answers about pricing.

### 6. Consultation Before Treatment
For hair colour, keratin, or any chemical treatment, a good salon will ask about your hair history before proceeding. This isn't just good practice — it protects you from damage.

### 7. Comfort & Communication
You should feel comfortable asking questions and saying no to upsells. A salon that respects your preferences and budget is one you'll keep returning to.
