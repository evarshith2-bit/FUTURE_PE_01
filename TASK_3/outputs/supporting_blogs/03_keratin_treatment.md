## What is Keratin Hair Treatment?

Keratin is a natural protein found in your hair. Over time, heat styling, chemical treatments, and environmental damage deplete it — leaving hair frizzy, dull, and difficult to manage.

A keratin treatment replenishes this protein by coating each hair strand with a keratin formula, which is then sealed in with heat. The result is smoother, shinier, and significantly more manageable hair — often within a single session.

## How Long Does Keratin Treatment Last in Hyderabad?

A professionally done keratin treatment typically lasts **3–5 months** depending on:
- How often you wash your hair (more washes = faster fade)
- Whether you use sulfate-free shampoo (highly recommended post-treatment)
- Your hair's natural texture and condition

Hyderabad's humidity can affect how quickly the treatment wears off — using the right aftercare products makes a significant difference.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is keratin treatment safe for colour-treated hair in Hyderabad?**
A: Yes, but timing matters. It's generally recommended to wait 2–3 weeks after colouring before doing a keratin treatment. Always consult your stylist.

**Q: Can I wash my hair immediately after a keratin treatment?**
A: No. Most keratin treatments require you to avoid washing your hair for 48–72 hours after the session to allow the formula to fully bond.

**Q: Does keratin treatment damage hair over time?**
A: A quality keratin treatment from a reputable salon actually improves hair health. Damage typically comes from low-quality products or over-processing.
