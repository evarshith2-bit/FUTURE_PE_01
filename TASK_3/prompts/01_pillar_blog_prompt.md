# Prompt 01 — Pillar Blog Generator

## Purpose
Generate a long-form, SEO-optimized pillar blog that targets the primary keyword, builds topical authority, and drives organic traffic from Google.

---

## What is a Pillar Blog?
A pillar blog is the main, comprehensive article that:
- Targets the highest-volume primary keyword
- Covers the topic deeply (1,500–2,500 words)
- Links out to all supporting blogs in the cluster
- Ranks for multiple related keywords

---

## Modular Prompt Template

```
You are an expert SEO content writer who writes for local business websites. Your content ranks on Google because it is helpful, structured, and matches search intent exactly.

Write a full pillar blog for the following business:

Business Name: {{business_name}}
Business Type: {{business_type}}
Location: {{location}}
Primary Keyword: {{primary_keyword}}
Target Audience: {{target_audience}}
Services to Cover: {{services}}
Tone: {{tone}}

Write a complete pillar blog with this exact structure:

H1: [Primary keyword naturally included — max 65 characters]

Introduction (150 words):
- Hook the reader with a relatable problem or question
- Include the primary keyword in the first 100 words
- Tell the reader what they'll learn

H2: Why [Location] Women Trust [Business Name]
- 3–4 sentences about what makes this salon different
- Include a trust signal (years of experience, trained staff, etc.)

H2: Our Complete List of Salon Services in [Location]
H3: Hair Services
H3: Skin & Facial Treatments
H3: Bridal & Occasion Packages
H3: Spa & Wellness
- Each H3: 2–3 sentences describing the service + benefit

H2: Salon Prices in [Location] — What to Expect
- General price range overview (no specific numbers, keep flexible)
- Value framing ("what you get for your money")

H2: How to Book an Appointment at [Business Name]
- Simple 3-step process
- Include a soft CTA

H2: Frequently Asked Questions
- 4 FAQs based on common Google searches for this business type

Conclusion (100 words):
- Summarize key points
- Include primary keyword once
- End with a strong CTA to visit or book

Internal Linking Suggestions:
- Suggest 4 anchor text links to supporting blog topics

SEO Notes:
- Use the primary keyword 4–6 times naturally
- Include location ("Hyderabad") in at least 3 headings
- Write in a helpful, conversational tone — not keyword-stuffed
```

## Filled Example — Velvet Touch Salon & Spa

```
Business Name: Velvet Touch Salon & Spa
Business Type: Premium full-service salon and spa
Location: Hyderabad, Telangana
Primary Keyword: best salon in Hyderabad
Target Audience: Women 20–45 in Hyderabad looking for hair, skin, and bridal services
Services to Cover: Hair treatments, bridal makeup, facials, keratin, nail art, spa
Tone: Helpful, warm, expert — like advice from a trusted beauty professional

[Apply full prompt structure above]
```

---

## Why This Prompt Works

| Element | SEO Reason |
|---|---|
| **Keyword in H1** | Strongest on-page SEO signal |
| **Keyword in first 100 words** | Google reads introductions first |
| **H2/H3 structure** | Improves crawlability and featured snippets |
| **FAQ section** | Targets "People Also Ask" boxes on Google |
| **Internal linking suggestions** | Builds topical authority across the cluster |
| **Location in headings** | Captures local search traffic |
