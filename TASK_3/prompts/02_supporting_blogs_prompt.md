# Prompt 02 — Supporting Blogs (Content Cluster)

## Purpose
Generate 5 supporting blog articles that target specific long-tail keywords, link back to the pillar blog, and build topical authority for the entire website.

---

## Modular Prompt Template

```
You are an expert SEO content writer. Write a supporting blog article for a local business website. This article is part of a content cluster and must link back to the main pillar blog.

Business Name: {{business_name}}
Location: {{location}}
Blog Topic: {{blog_topic}}
Primary Keyword: {{primary_keyword}}
Search Intent: {{search_intent}}
Pillar Blog Title: {{pillar_blog_title}}
Tone: {{tone}}

Write a complete supporting blog with this structure:

H1: [Primary keyword naturally included — max 65 characters]

Introduction (100 words):
- Open with the reader's question or problem
- Include primary keyword in first 50 words
- Brief preview of what the article covers

H2: [Main subtopic 1]
H3: [Sub-point if needed]
- 150–200 words of helpful, specific content

H2: [Main subtopic 2]
- 150–200 words

H2: [Main subtopic 3]
- 150–200 words

H2: Frequently Asked Questions
- 3 FAQs matching "People Also Ask" style searches

Conclusion (80 words):
- Summarize key takeaway
- Internal link: "For a complete guide to salon services in [location], read our [Pillar Blog Title]"
- CTA to book or visit

SEO Notes:
- Use primary keyword 3–4 times naturally
- Include location in at least 2 headings
- Keep sentences short and scannable
- Match the search intent exactly
```

## Why This Prompt Works

| Element | SEO Reason |
|---|---|
| **Long-tail keyword targeting** | Less competition, higher conversion intent |
| **Search intent matching** | Google rewards content that answers what the user actually wants |
| **Internal link to pillar** | Passes link equity and builds cluster authority |
| **FAQ section** | Targets People Also Ask — free Google real estate |
| **Location in headings** | Captures "near me" and city-specific searches |
