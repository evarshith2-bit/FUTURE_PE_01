# Prompt 03 — Local SEO Optimization

## Purpose
Optimize any blog for local search by naturally weaving in city, neighborhood, and service+location keyword combinations that rank in Google's local results.

---

## Modular Prompt Template

```
You are a local SEO specialist. Your job is to take a blog article and optimize it for local search without making it feel keyword-stuffed or unnatural.

Business Name: {{business_name}}
Business Type: {{business_type}}
Primary City: {{city}}
Neighborhoods / Areas to Target: {{neighborhoods}}
Primary Service Keyword: {{service_keyword}}
Current Blog Title: {{blog_title}}

Perform the following local SEO optimizations:

1. Meta Title (max 60 characters)
   - Include primary keyword + city
   - Example: "Best Salon in Hyderabad | Velvet Touch Spa"

2. Meta Description (max 155 characters)
   - Include keyword + city + clear benefit + soft CTA
   - Example: "Looking for the best salon in Hyderabad? Velvet Touch offers hair, bridal & spa services in Banjara Hills. Book today."

3. Suggest 3 H2 heading rewrites that include location naturally
   - Before and after versions

4. Local Keyword Variations to weave into the article (10 suggestions)
   - Format: keyword — where to place it in the article

5. Google Business Profile Description (150 words)
   - Written to attract clicks from Google Maps results
   - Include top 3 services + location + trust signal + CTA

6. Schema Markup Suggestions
   - List 3 schema types relevant to this business
   - Briefly explain what each does for SEO
```

## Filled Example — Velvet Touch Salon & Spa

```
Business Name: Velvet Touch Salon & Spa
Business Type: Premium full-service salon and spa
Primary City: Hyderabad
Neighborhoods to Target: Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Kondapur, Madhapur, Gachibowli
Service Keyword: best salon in Hyderabad
Blog Title: Best Salon in Hyderabad – Services, Prices & What to Expect

[Apply full prompt above]
```

## Local Keyword Matrix — Velvet Touch Salon & Spa

| Keyword Type | Example Keywords |
|---|---|
| **Service + City** | salon in Hyderabad, spa in Hyderabad |
| **Service + Neighborhood** | salon in Banjara Hills, spa in Jubilee Hills |
| **Specific Service + City** | bridal makeup Hyderabad, keratin treatment Hyderabad |
| **Near Me Intent** | salon near me Hyderabad, best spa near Kondapur |
| **Comparison Intent** | best salon in Hyderabad, top rated spa Hyderabad |
| **Price Intent** | salon price list Hyderabad, facial cost Hyderabad |

---

## Why This Prompt Works

| Element | Local SEO Reason |
|---|---|
| **Meta title with city** | First thing Google and users see in search results |
| **Meta description** | Improves click-through rate from search results |
| **Neighborhood targeting** | Captures hyper-local "near me" searches |
| **Google Business Profile** | Optimizes for Google Maps — huge local traffic source |
| **Schema markup** | Helps Google understand business type, location, reviews |
