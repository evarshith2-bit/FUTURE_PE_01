# Prompt 01 — Multiple Hooks Generator

## Purpose
Generate 8 scroll-stopping hooks for the same product — different angles, same goal: stop the viewer in the first 3 seconds.

---

## Why Hooks Matter
The first 3 seconds of a UGC ad determine everything. A great hook = viewer watches. A weak hook = instant swipe. You need multiple hooks to A/B test which angle converts best for your audience.

---

## Modular Prompt Template

```
You are a UGC ad scriptwriter and conversion copywriter. Your job is to write scroll-stopping hooks for short-form video ads. Hooks must feel real and human — like something a genuine customer would say on camera, not a brand announcement.

Brand: {{brand_name}}
Product: {{product_name}}
Core Benefit: {{product_benefit}}
Target Audience: {{target_audience}}
Pain Point: {{pain_point}}
Platform: {{platform}}
Tone: {{tone}}

Write 8 different hooks for this product. Each hook should:
- Be 1–2 sentences max
- Take no more than 3 seconds to say out loud
- Use a different angle (curiosity, pain point, result, controversy, relatability, question, story, social proof)
- Feel like something a real person would say on camera
- NOT start with the brand name

Label each hook with its angle type.
```

---

## Filled Example — GlowLab Vitamin C Serum

```
You are a UGC ad scriptwriter and conversion copywriter. Write scroll-stopping hooks for short-form video ads. Hooks must feel real and human — like something a genuine customer would say on camera.

Brand: GlowLab Skincare
Product: Vitamin C Brightening Serum
Core Benefit: Visibly brighter skin in 14 days
Target Audience: Women 18–35 with dull skin or dark spots
Pain Point: Dull, uneven skin tone despite trying many products
Platform: Instagram Reels
Tone: Honest, relatable, conversational — like a friend's recommendation

Write 8 hooks, each using a different angle: curiosity, pain point, result, controversy, relatability, question, story, social proof. Label each. Max 2 sentences per hook.
```

---

## Why This Prompt Works

| Element | Reason |
|---|---|
| **8 hooks, 8 angles** | Gives variety for A/B testing — brands need options |
| **"Not the brand name"** | Forces natural UGC opening, not a branded announcement |
| **3-second rule** | Anchors the AI to real platform behavior |
| **Angle labels** | Makes it easy to pick the right hook for the right audience |

---

## Hook Angles — Quick Reference

| Angle | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Curiosity** | Makes viewer need to know more |
| **Pain Point** | Hits the exact frustration the viewer feels |
| **Result** | Leads with the outcome, pulls in aspirational viewers |
| **Controversy** | Challenges a common belief — creates stops |
| **Relatability** | "Same girl" energy — viewer sees themselves |
| **Question** | Directly asks the viewer something personal |
| **Story** | Opens a loop that needs to be closed |
| **Social Proof** | Numbers, dermatologists, reviews build instant trust |
