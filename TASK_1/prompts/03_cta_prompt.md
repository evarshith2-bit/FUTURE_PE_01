# Prompt 03 — CTA Sections

## Purpose
Generate call-to-action sections that drive bookings, visits, and enquiries — with trust-building copy and strong button text.

---

## Modular Prompt Template (Fill Variables Before Use)

```
You are a conversion copywriter. Write CTA sections for a {{business_type}} website. The CTAs must feel genuine and warm — not pushy or salesy.

Business Name: {{business_name}}
Location: {{location}}
Primary Goals: {{goal_1}}, {{goal_2}}, {{goal_3}}
Tone: {{tone}}

Write the following CTA sections:

1. Visit Us CTA
   - Heading (max 8 words)
   - 2 sentences encouraging a first visit
   - Address + opening hours placeholder
   - Button text (max 4 words, action-oriented)

2. Book / Enquire CTA
   - Heading (max 8 words)
   - 2 sentences for reservation or enquiry
   - One trust line removing friction
   - Button text (max 4 words, action-oriented)

3. Stay Connected CTA
   - Heading (max 8 words)
   - 1–2 sentences inviting social follow
   - Button text (max 4 words)

4. Footer Trust Statements
   - 3 micro-copy lines (max 6 words each) building credibility

Format clearly with section labels.
```

---

## Filled Example — Brewed & Beloved Café

```
You are a conversion copywriter. Write CTA sections for a café website. The CTAs must feel genuine and warm — not pushy or salesy.

Business Name: Brewed & Beloved Café
Location: Hyderabad, Telangana
Primary Goals: Get visitors to visit the café, book a table or private event, follow on Instagram
Tone: Friendly, warm, community-driven — make visitors feel welcomed, not sold to

Write: Visit Us CTA, Book a Table CTA, Stay Connected CTA, Footer Trust Statements.
Format clearly with section labels. Button text max 4 words, action-oriented.
```

---

## Why This Prompt Works

| Element | Reason |
|---|---|
| **"Not pushy or salesy"** | Shifts tone to community-first copy |
| **Button text limit (4 words)** | Forces punchy, clickable action phrases |
| **Trust line instruction** | Adds social proof and removes purchase anxiety |
| **Footer trust section** | Produces micro-copy often overlooked by AI |

---

## Strong vs Weak Button Text

| Weak | Strong |
|---|---|
| Find Us Here | Plan Your Visit |
| Book Now | Reserve Your Table |
| Follow Us | See Daily Specials |
| Contact Us | Start a Conversation |
