# Prompt 01 — Homepage Copy

## Purpose
Generate homepage copy for a local business with a strong headline, sub-headline, value proposition, and intro section.

---

## Modular Prompt Template (Fill Variables Before Use)

```
You are a professional website copywriter specializing in local businesses. Your writing is warm, persuasive, and conversion-focused — never generic.

Write homepage copy for the following business:

Business Name: {{business_name}}
Business Type: {{business_type}}
Location: {{location}}
Target Audience: {{target_audience}}
Unique Selling Points:
  - {{usp_1}}
  - {{usp_2}}
  - {{usp_3}}
Tone: {{tone}}

Write the following sections:
1. Headline (max 10 words — strong value proposition)
2. Sub-headline (1–2 sentences — who it's for and what they'll feel)
3. Intro Section (3–4 sentences — tell the story of the business, make it feel real and inviting)

Format the output clearly with section labels.
```

---

## Filled Example — Brewed & Beloved Café

```
You are a professional website copywriter specializing in local businesses. Your writing is warm, persuasive, and conversion-focused — never generic.

Business Name: Brewed & Beloved Café
Business Type: Specialty coffee café and all-day brunch spot
Location: Hyderabad, Telangana
Target Audience: Young professionals, students, remote workers, and families
Unique Selling Points:
  - Specialty single-origin coffee
  - Freshly made all-day brunch menu with locally sourced ingredients
  - Work-friendly space — Wi-Fi, charging points, cozy corners
Tone: Warm, friendly, inviting — like a message from a friend who knows the best spots in the city

Write: Headline + Sub-headline + Intro Section. Format with section labels.
```

---

## Why This Prompt Works

| Element | Reason |
|---|---|
| **Role assignment** | Anchors the AI in expert copywriter mode |
| **`{{variables}}`** | Makes the prompt modular and reusable for any client |
| **USPs listed explicitly** | AI weaves them naturally instead of being generic |
| **Tone directive** | Produces warmer, more human text |
| **Word/sentence limits** | Keeps output tight and website-ready |
